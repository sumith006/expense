TECHNICAL DETAILS
================================================================================

DATA SOURCES:
  • Hive 'expenses' box → expense data
  • Hive 'incomes' box → income data
  • Hive 'tasks' box → task data

CHARTS USED:
  • LineChart (fl_chart) - Income vs Expenses trend
  • PieChart (fl_chart) - Category spending distribution
  • CircularProgressIndicator - Task completion
  • LinearProgressIndicator - Priority breakdown

THEME:
  • NeoBrutalTheme colors and styling
  • Dark mode compatible
  • Professional appearance
  • Consistent with app design

CALCULATIONS:
  • Total Income = sum of all incomes
  • Total Expenses = sum of all expenses
  • Net Savings = income - expenses
  • Savings Rate = (savings / income) × 100%
  • Task Completion Rate = (completed / total) × 100%
  • Category totals = sum by category
  • Monthly trends = grouped by month/year
